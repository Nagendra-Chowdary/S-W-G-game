Here’s how to play the Snake, Water, Gun game — it’s similar to Rock, Paper, Scissors:


🔁 Basic Rules

Players: Two players (or you vs computer).
Choices: Each player picks one of the three — Snake, Water, or Gun.
Win Conditions:
Snake drinks Water → Snake wins 🐍💧
Water douses Gun → Water wins 💧🔫
Gun kills Snake → Gun wins 🔫🐍
Same choice → It's a Draw

🕹️ How to Play

The computer and player secretly chooses one option.
Both reveal their choice at the same time.
The winner is determined based on the rules above.
